Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and large events, we recommend booking 2-3 months in advance. Corporate events and smaller gatherings can often be accommodated with 2-3 weeks notice, subject to availability.

Do you cater events outside Abakaliki?

Yes! While we're based in Abakaliki, we serve clients across Ebonyi State and neighboring communities. Delivery fees apply based on distance.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We regularly prepare meals for guests with allergies, religious dietary requirements, vegetarian preferences, and medical restrictions. Just inform us during consultation.

What's included in your catering packages?

Our packages typically include menu planning, food preparation, serving equipment, staff (for full-service events), and cleanup. We customize based on your specific needs and budget.

How do payments work?

We require a 40% deposit to secure your date, with the balance due 72 hours before your event. We accept bank transfers, cash, and mobile money payments.

Do you provide tasting sessions?

Yes, for wedding and premium packages, we offer complimentary tasting sessions so you can sample and approve your menu before the big day.
